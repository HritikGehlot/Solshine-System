Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about solar installation

How long does solar installation take?

Most residential installations are completed in 1-2 days. The entire process from consultation to activation typically takes 4-8 weeks, including permitting and utility interconnection.

How much can I save with solar?

Most customers save 70-90% on their electricity bills. Exact savings depend on your energy usage, roof size, local electricity rates, and available incentives.

What financing options do you offer?

We offer cash purchases, solar loans with $0 down, and leasing options. We'll help you choose the best financing solution for your budget and goals.

Do solar panels work during power outages?

Standard solar systems shut down during outages for safety. However, systems with battery storage can provide backup power during outages.

What maintenance do solar panels require?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. We recommend annual inspections and occasional cleaning. Our maintenance plans ensure optimal performance.

How long do solar panels last?

Quality solar panels last 25-30 years with minimal degradation. We offer 25-year warranties and expect systems to continue producing power well beyond that.
